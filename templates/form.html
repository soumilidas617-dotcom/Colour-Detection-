Understanding Spam & Staying Safe

Spam refers to unwanted or irrelevant digital messages — often used to promote products, spread misinformation, or deliver harmful links and malware.

Tips to Stay Safe:

  • Never click on unknown links or attachments.
  • Verify the sender’s email before responding.
  • Use spam filters and keep your software updated.
  • Report suspicious messages immediately.
